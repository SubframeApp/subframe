Subframe offers three plans: Free, Pro, and Custom. Plans are billed per team, not per user. Viewers are always free—only Editor and Admin seats cost money on paid plans.

## Available plans

**Free** - $0/month forever. Start building with 1 project, 5 pages, 1 prototype, and unlimited team members. Perfect for trying Subframe or small personal projects.

**Pro** - $29/month per editor. Unlimited projects, pages, and prototypes. Full AI features and 7-day version history. Add as many Viewers as you want for free.

**Custom** - Contact sales. Unlimited everything plus custom AI training, import existing components, and dedicated Slack support. For large teams and agencies.

## How billing works

### Seat-based pricing

Only Editors and Admins count as paid seats. Viewers are free regardless of how many you add.

**Example:**
- 3 Admins = $87/month
- 5 Editors = $145/month
- 20 Viewers = $0/month
- **Total: $232/month**

### When you're charged

Billing updates automatically when you add or remove Editors/Admins. Changes take effect in your next billing cycle—you won't be charged immediately for new seats.

If you remove an Editor or change them to a Viewer, you'll stop paying for that seat in the next billing cycle.

### Billing cycle

Pro plan bills monthly. Your billing date is set when you first subscribe and repeats each month.

You can view upcoming charges and billing dates in the Stripe billing portal (accessible from **Team Settings > Billing**).

## Upgrading to Pro

1. Navigate to **Team Settings** from your user menu
2. Click **Upgrade to Pro**
3. You'll be redirected to Stripe checkout
4. Enter payment information
5. Confirm your subscription

Your plan upgrades immediately after payment. You can start using Pro features right away.

The checkout automatically calculates the cost based on how many Editors and Admins are currently on your team.

## Managing your subscription

### Access billing portal

Navigate to **Team Settings > Billing** and click **Manage subscription**. This opens Stripe's billing portal where you can:

- Update payment methods
- View invoices
- See upcoming charges
- Cancel your subscription

### Changing payment methods

In the billing portal:
1. Click **Update payment method**
2. Enter new card details
3. Save changes

Your next charge will use the new payment method.

### Canceling subscription

You can cancel anytime from the billing portal. Click **Cancel subscription** and confirm.

When you cancel:
- Access to Pro features continues until the end of your billing period
- Your plan automatically downgrades to Free when the period ends
- All Editors and Admins become Viewers
- Projects beyond the Free plan limit become read-only

<Warning>
Canceling removes access to unlimited projects and pages. If you have more than 1 project or 5 pages, you'll need to delete extras or keep them in read-only mode.
</Warning>

## Free trial

New teams get a 14-day free trial when they sign up. During the trial, your account is on the Free plan. The trial period doesn't provide Pro features—it's a grace period to explore Subframe.

After 14 days, you'll need to upgrade to Pro if you want unlimited projects and full AI features.

## Viewer seats are free

Viewers never cost money, on any plan. Add unlimited Viewers to your team without affecting your bill.

Only Editors and Admins count toward your monthly cost on the Pro plan.

## Downgrading

To downgrade from Pro to Free, cancel your subscription in the billing portal. Your plan reverts to Free at the end of the billing period.

When downgrading:
- Keep 1 project and 5 pages (delete extras or they become read-only)
- All Editors and Admins become Viewers
- AI features become limited
- Version history reduces to 24 hours
