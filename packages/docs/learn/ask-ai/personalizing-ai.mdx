title: Personalizing AI
---

Add project context so AI generates designs with relevant copy and appropriate styling for your product. AI personalization settings are project-specific.

## Set your project context

1. Click the **gear icon** in the Ask AI chat bar, or click your project name in the navbar and select **Project settings**
2. Enter your **Company name**
3. Enter your **project description** â€”describe your company, product, and design preferences
4. Click **Save** to apply your settings

Ask AI references your project context for all generations.

## What to include

- What your company does
- Who your target users are
- Your product's key features
- Preferred tone and voice
- Industry-specific terminology
- Design guidelines referencing your components and theme
